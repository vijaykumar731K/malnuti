How the AI screening works

Designed for busy clinics and field visits — NutriSight AI guides you through a simple, standardized workflow to capture reliable data.

📷

Upload child image

Capture a front-facing image with consent. The AI analyzes facial cues and visible anthropometric markers.

📊

Enter health parameters

Add age, gender, height, weight and MUAC. The system validates ranges and flags any data entry errors.

🤖

AI-powered analysis

Deep models benchmark each child against WHO Z-scores and regional cohorts for more contextual insights.

✅

Risk prediction & advice

Get risk levels (Healthy / Moderate / Severe) plus tailored nutrition and follow-up recommendations.
