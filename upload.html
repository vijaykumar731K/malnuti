Tips for accurate screening

Follow these simple steps so the AI can provide the most reliable result.

  • Measure height and weight twice and use the average.
  • Ensure MUAC tape is placed at the mid-point of the upper arm.
  • Use good lighting and avoid strong shadows on the child’s face.
  • Keep the child’s head in a neutral, front-facing position.
