AI-guided nutrition recommendations

These sample recommendations are tailored around a 2–5 year old child with moderate malnutrition risk. Always adapt to local foods and cultural preferences.

Weekly diet suggestion

Balanced, nutrient-dense meals that can be prepared with common ingredients in Indian households.

Mon
Breakfast: Vegetable upma with peanuts
Lunch: Dal, rice, ghee & curd
Snack: Banana with groundnut chikki
Tue
Breakfast: Egg omelette & chapati
Lunch: Khichdi with vegetables
Snack: Roasted chana & jaggery
Wed
Breakfast: Idli with sambar
Lunch: Fish/soy curry & rice
Snack: Curd with seasonal fruits
Thu
Breakfast: Poha with vegetables & peanuts
Lunch: Chapati, dal & leafy sabzi
Snack: Milk with homemade laddoo
Fri
Breakfast: Sprouted moong chaat
Lunch: Curd rice & vegetable curry
Snack: Boiled egg or paneer cubes
Sat / Sun
Mix of the above meals + one favourite dish to improve appetite.
